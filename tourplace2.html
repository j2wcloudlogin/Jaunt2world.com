Romance in Sri Lanka: Special Honeymoon Package

6 Days & 5 Nights

Overview

Have the most fun-filled and blissful honeymoon with a 5 nights, 6 days Sri Lanka honeymoon packagethat will help you create everlasting memories with your loved one. Set on for a mesmeric vacation with your soulmate to the captivating expanses of this island nation. This6 day Sri Lanka itinerarystrengthens your bond while exploring these breathtaking destinations of Sri Lanka.

Sri Lanka can be a perfect destination for all those couples who love nature, serenity, peace, culture, historical stories and mythologies. Sri Lanka has all of the above stated things in abundance which is surely going to fascinate you and make your honeymoon worth remembering. Sri Lanka besides its rich and cultural heritage is also famous for its spa and rejuvenating wellness packages which is a must to take on your honeymoon to pacify your soul and mind after those hectic wedding rituals. The bazaars in Sri Lanka are quirky exhibiting the real culture and traditions of the country in everything it sells.6 days Sri Lanka honeymoon itinerary can be customized as per your liking and requirements.

Kickstart your honeymoon in Sri Lanka with arrival at Colombo from where you are transferred to Sigiriya. Visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage, where elephants are taken care of in the best manner. Your Sigiriya sightseeing further includes Dambulla Cave Temple,Sigiriya Rock Fortress, introducing you to the age old marvels of Sri Lanka. From Sigiriya, your “Sri Lanka honeymoon itinerary “proceeds to Kandy from where you will be transferred to the scenic Buddhist site. Heighten your honeymooning delight in the picturesque and charming Nuwara Eliya, have a fun carnival at the beaches of Bentota, and lastly, the happening Colombo.

You and your beloved will enjoy this charming “Sri Lankan honeymoon package” that can be customized according to your preferences. Make the most of this romantic honeymoon package in Sri Lanka and create beautiful memories for the first time with your significant other. Go for romantic walks on the beach, enjoy the beautiful scenery around with your beloved and visit sacred relics in this beautiful place. Book this5 nights, 6 days Sri Lanka honeymoon now for a fun-filled, hassle-free romantic journey of your new life.

Cities:

• Sigiriya (1D)

• Kandy (1D)

• Nuwara Eliya (1D)

• Bentota (1D)

• Colombo (2D)

Day 1

Sigiriya: Arrival in Colombo

Other Benefits (On Arrival)

Cab Airport TransferSightseeing

Start your Sri Lankan honeymoon tour in the ancient rock fortress of Sigiriya

An agent’s representative will receive you, once you arrive at the Bandaranaike International Airport in Colombo and will also help you with your transfers to the hotel in Sigiriya. En route, you will get to visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage and witness innocent baby elephants being fed by caretakers. You can also visit Dambulla Cave Temple that has 80 ancient caves. On the way, you will get to enjoy the experience of climbing 1,200 steps that lead to the top of Sigiriya Rock Fortress. Also visit Water Garden, Frescoes, Mirror Wall, and Lions Paw at Sigiriya.

Once you reach Sigiriya, check into the hotel and then stay for the night.

Tip: Make sure you wear clothing that covers your legs and shoulders while visiting temples in Sri Lanka. Carry a sarong or a scarf for the same if needed

Distance from Colombo to Sigiriya: approx 180 km

Travel Time: approx. 4 hours

Day 2

Kandy: Off to the heritage city

Other Benefits (On Arrival)

BreakfastSightseeingCab Transfer

p>Start the Day 2 of your Sri Lanka honeymoon package with a transfer to the beautiful Kandy

After a hearty breakfast, get transferred to the beautiful city of Kandy. Enjoy the scenic views en route and once you reach, check into the hotel. Rest for a while and then set out and explore the beautiful Tooth Relic Temple, where the sacred relic of the Buddha’s tooth is.

Take your beloved’s hand and head over to the Ceylon Tea Museum and let the wonderful aroma embrace both of you. Next, head over to Kandy Lake and just sit back and relax. You can also buy some knickknacks for each other at Market Square. After a fun day, head back to the hotel and stay the night.

Optional:

If you have time, request for a short trip to Kitulgala and indulge in a bit of whitewater rafting. (charges extra)

Distance from Sigiriya to Kandy: approx 90 km

Travel Time: approx 2.5 hours

Day 3

Nuwara Eliya: Sightseeing tour

Other Benefits (On Arrival)

BreakfastSightseeingCab Transfer

Enhance your romance on Day 3 of your 6 day Sri Lanka itinerary with a trip to the charming town of Nuwara Eliya.

Start the day with a delicious breakfast with your loved one and then check out from the hotel. Set out to explore the English ambiance of the rose-tinted Nuwara Eliya town on your honeymoon trip in Sri Lanka.

Enjoy a fun scenic ride with your beloved through the estates and then head over to Peradeniya Botanical Garden. Stop at the Ramboda Waterfalls and click pictures of you and your better half under these majestic falls.

Once you have spent a fun day with each other, check into the hotel and stay for the night.

Distance from Kandy to Nuwara Eliya: approx 76 km

Travel Time: approx 2.5 hours

Day 4

Bentota: Beach walks & much more

Other Benefits (On Arrival)

BreakfastCab TransferSightseeing

Vist the lively and vibrant town of Bentota on the fourth day of your Sri Lanka honeymoon itinerary

Start the day with a hearty breakfast and then check out from the hotel. You and your loved one will then be transferred to Bentota. Enjoy the scenic views en route as you journey through to Bentota on this honeymoon trip to Sri Lanka.

Once you reach Bentota, check into the resort and then head over to the beach. Take romantic walks on the beach and go fishing. You can also take part in interacting with the cute turtles at the Turtle Hatchery project. Once you have spent your time at the beach, head back to the hotel and rest.

Distance from Nuwara Eliya to Bentota:approx 210 km

Travel time:approx 5 hours

Day 5

Colombo: Welcome to the capital city

Other Benefits (On Arrival)

Breakfast Cab Transfer Sightseeing

Explore the beautiful ocean city of Colombo

Start your day with a delicious breakfast and then check out from the hotel. Whisper sweet nothings in your beloved’s ears en route to the garden city of the east, Colombo.

Visit the Galle Face Green and just rest on the vast lawns. Then see the beauty of the Dutch architecture at the Old City Hall and the Dutch Period Museum.

Distance from Bentota to Colombo: approx 87 kmTravel time: approx 1 hour

Your Sri Lanka itinerary comes to an end but the memories will last forever.
