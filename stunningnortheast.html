DAY WISE ITINERARY

DAY 1: IXB AIRPORT / NJP RAILWAY STATION TO DARJEELING (ALT 7100FT), 90KM IN 3HRS.

Assistance at IXB Airport/ NJP railway station on arrival and drive to Darjeeling. Check in at Hotel. Evening is free at leisure. Overnight stay will be at Darjeeling.

✈**********************************************************************************************************************

DAY 2: DARJEELING LOCAL SIGHTSEEING.

After breakfast start at 8.30 am visit Himalayan Mountaineering Institute, Padmaja Naidu Zoological Park (Thursday closed), Tenzing Rock, Ropeway, Tibetan Refugee self-help Centre (Sunday closed), Tea Garden (outer view), Japanese Temple, Ghoom Monastery, Batasia loop, Tiger hill (8,400 ft.). Evening free at leisure. Overnight stay at Darjeeling

Optional tour of Toy Train (Joy Ride) can also be arranged on advance request and availability of the tickets at an additional cost.

✈**********************************************************************************************************************

DAY 3: DARJEELING TO GANGTOK (ALT 5500FT /1650M) 105KMS IN 4HRS + GANGTOK HALF DAY SIGHT SEEING

After breakfast drive to Gangtok (Capital of Sikkim). Gangtok is also a resting ground for people traveling to North Sikkim. Surrounded by Monasteries and Orchids, Gangtok is truly a traveler’s delight. Check in at Hotel and then proceed for a half day Gangtok city tour. Visit Bakthang Falls, Enchey Monastery, Flower Show, Sikkim Research Institute of Tibetology, Dodurl Chorten andHandicraft Centre. Evening is free at leisure. Overnight stay will be at Gangtok.

✈**********************************************************************************************************************

DAY 4: EXCURSION TRIP TO TSOMGO LAKE + BABA MANDIR

Day excursion trip of TSOMGO Lake (12,400 ft.) & Baba Harbhajan Singh Memorial (13200 ft) 52 kms from Gangtok city. TSOMGO Lake freezes completely during the winter and is much revered by the locals. Overnight stay will be at Gangtok.

"Optional tour" to Nathula Pass (Alt 14000Ft / 4200M) on China Border can also be arranged (Except Mon, Tue) for Indian Nationals on advance request at an additional cost.

✈**********************************************************************************************************************

DAY 5: GANGTOK TO IXB AIRPORT / NJP RAILWAY STATION 130KMS IN 4HRS

Morning free for packing & personal activities. Check out from hotel and drive to NJP/Bagdogra.

✈**********************************************************************************************************************
