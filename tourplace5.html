    Tour Overview

Best of Vietnam and Cambodia Tour

5 Nights 6 Days Tour Package

Day 1

SIEM REAP ARRIVAL – ROLOUS GROUP - ANGKOR WAT

Upon arrival in Siem Reap International Airport, the tour guide of Deluxe Vietnam Tours will meet you and transfer you to the hotel for check in. Start to explore the early Angkor-era temples of the Roluos, the first city created by the kings of Cambodia inclusive of Bakong, Preah Ko and Lolei temples.

In the afternoon, you will explore the Amazing “Angkor Wat temple” which is the top of all Cambodia tour packages. It was built in the early 12th century by King Suryavarman II. Your tour guide will share how this masterpiece of Khmer architecture was built and explain the different motifs. Please feel free to question your professional expert. Overnight in Siem Reap.

Day 2

SIEM REAP – ANGKOR THOM – BANTEAY SREY

After breakfast at the hotel, start your full day Angkor Wat tour with the Angkor Thom. 1st visit to The South Gate of Angkor Thom. Then, you will get crazy with famous giant stone faces Bayon temple. It was built in late 12th early 13th century by the King Jayavarman VII. Continue your Angkor wat tour with The Baphuon temple - the huge temple-mountain in the heart of Angkor Thom. Then, explore some other masterpieces built in 12th century by the King Jayavarman VII - Phimean Akas temple and other two Leper King Terrace and Elephant Terrace.

Your afternoon Angkor tour will be exciting with the charming Banteay Srei temple Which is well-known as the Citadel of Women. Here, you can see the most exquisite carvings in the Khmer architecture. End your Angkor wat tour today with another temple – Banteay Samré before getting back to the hotel. Overnight in Siem Reap.

Day 3

SIEM REAP – TONLE SAP LAKE – HANOI

After breakfast at the hotel, your tour guide and driver will pick up you for the Tonle Sap Lake boat trip. Explore the fisherman life and the floating village in the Great Lake of Cambodia and biggest in South-east Asia. Then, transfer to the airport for departure to Hanoi - Vietnam.

Enjoy a short flight from Siemreap - Cambodia to Hanoi Vietnam. Arriving at Noibai airport, you will get welcomed by the local guide of Deluxe Vietnam Tours for the transfer to your hotel in Hanoi. OVernight in Ha Noi.

Day 4

Hanoi to Halong bay

Enjoy Vietnamese and Western buffet at your hotel before getting picked up for the journey to Ha Long bay for 2 days/ 1 night tour - the journey to the Heaven. Driving through the countryside in the north of Vietnam, you will see the big difference between Vietnam and Cambodia. Getting to Halong bay, you will be welcomed by the cruise manager and check in.

Day 5

Halong bay - Top of Vietnam Cambodia package tour

UNESCO twice voted Halong bay as World Natural Hesitage Site and Halong bay was voted by travellers as One of 7 New Natural Wonders. This could tell you how beautiful it is. The bay is also listed as one of the places must be visited before die.

The best way to explore the breathtaking bay is to join a cruise from tight budget to luxury( from 1 cabin cruise for honey moon trip to 40 cabin cruise for family and company trip).

Day 6

Hanoi depature

Upon your flight time of flying out of Vietnam, customize Hanoi city tour with the most highlights for amazing experience from bustling Hanoi Old Quarter with rickshaw tour, wooden puppets dance on top of water.... to Ho Chi Minh complex where Vietnamese leader is being seen. The end of 6 day Cambodia Vietnam tour package with Deluxe Vietnam Tours Company(B)
