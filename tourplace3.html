A Delightful Singapore and Malaysia Tour Package

6 Nights & 7 Days

Overview

Take up Singapore packages if you want to have an amazing and unforgettable vacation. Every Singapore tour package is customized as per your pocket and preferences. Couple a Singapore trip with a short visit to Kuala Lumpur for unlimited doses of fun.

Here Singapore Malaysia packages serve the best purpose. Transform your holiday from a routine vacation into a dynamic one with a Singapore and Malaysia tour package. It is lucrative and all inclusive with meals, transfers, sightseeing, and accommodation. Loaded with features such as accommodation, meals, roundtrip airport to hotel transfers, and tour of the city; your Singapore holiday package offers true value for money. In addition, every Singapore itinerary communicates the day-wise fun and excitement that you will be enjoying. Singapore, frequently flocked by tourists offers natural landmarks and contemporary establishments for sightseeing with families. Singapore cheap packages from India provide a pocket friendly and pleasurable trip to those who don’t want to splash their cash. No matter, from which Indian state you book a Singapore and Malaysia tour package, a trip to treasure is promised. You can choose from a plethora of Singapore packages from Ahmedabad, and even customize these as per your requirements. The best itinerary for Singapore vacation details the everyday activities you will be enjoying through your holiday with family.

Replete with abundance of natural beauty and popular attractions, an excursion through this 7 days Singapore and Malaysia tour package is an amalgamation of adventure and splendour that a tourist can get to cherish here.

Day 1

Singapore: Arrival in Singapore coupled by a visit to Night Safari

On your arrival in Singapore, an agent’s representative will meet and escort you to your hotel. After you check-into the hotel, relax and get ready for an exciting trip in the evening.

When the sun goes down, you will be visiting the world's first wildlife park at night- Night Safari. Watch how animals behave in their natural habitat when night falls.

Return to the hotel and have a relaxing stay overnight.

Day 2

Singapore: A delightful day of Singapore city tour with a trip to Sentosa Island

Savor a hearty breakfast at the hotel and proceed for an amazing tour of Singapore City. Visit Chinatown, Gem Factory, Little India, and Central Business District.

Meandering through the Orchard Road will bring you many stopovers to click photographs. To have a glance at the cultural heritage of Singapore, explore its myriad museums, The Esplanade – Theatres on the Bay, and Kampong Glam.

Once you have visited the major tourist attractions of the city, you will be taken to the tranquil Sentosa Island via SIC transfers and tickets. One way cable car, Underwater world, Dolphin Lagoon, and Songs of the sea are included in these tickets.

Come back to the hotel for peaceful overnight stay.

Day 3

Singapore: A unique Universal Studios trip

Once you have had your breakfast, be prepared to live the movies at Universal Studios - Asia’s only Hollywood movie theme park. This theme park is situated within the Resort World Sentosa.

Enjoy various movie-themed rides along with other irresistible attractions of this theme park. The rides and shows that are based on blockbuster movies will be enjoyed immensely by adventure lovers and families, alike.

Delight in the 4-D adventure presented by Shrek and his gang in first Far Far away castle of the world. Experience the ecstasy of warrior mummies through a psychological thrill ride - Revenge of the Mummy, in darkness. Also visit New York, The Lost World, Madagascar, Sci Fi City, and Hollywood.

Return to the hotel and stay overnight.

Day 4

Kuala Lumpur: Transfer to Kuala Lumpur and a city tour

Have a hearty breakfast before you leave for Kuala Lumpur for the next part of your exciting vacation. On your arrival in Kuala Lumpur, you will be greeted by the local tour operator’s representative.

Thereafter, you will be taken to your hotel. After you have checked-in, relax and refresh so that you will be energized for your visit to a wonderful city tour.

The tour takes you to amazing sightseeing attractions. You will be visiting National Monument, National Mosque, Independence Square, Thean Hou Temple, KL Tower, and Cocoa Boutique. Stopover at King's Palace and Petronas Twin Towers for some clickable moments.

Come back to your hotel after an action-packed day for a refreshing overnight stay.

Day 5

Kuala Lumpur: A fun-filled day at Sunway Lagoon

Savor a delectable breakfast and leave for Sunway Lagoon that is one-stop for non-stop fun and entertainment. Revel in the crucial theme park experience with more than 80 attractions sprawling across 88 acres of land.

Enjoy the rides of the Amusement Park with the little ones and have splashing moments at the Water Park. Interact with the animals at the Wildlife Park and gear up for more action at the Extreme Park followed by an experience to face your fears at Scream Park. Sunway Lagoon addresses all your fantasies!

Return to the hotel and stay overnight.

Day 6

Kuala Lumpur: Enjoy a tour to Genting

Have a hearty breakfast and leave for the next part of your exciting vacation. Check-out from the hotel and start for Genting.

En route, you will be greeted by many sightseeing attractions that include Batu Caves and one way cable car. Take time to explore these limestone caves that have religious significance and enjoy your cable car ride. Once you reach Genting, be prepared to visit ‘Snow World’ a theme park.

Return to KL and stay overnight at the hotel.

Day 7

Kuala Lumpur: Time to move back home

Have breakfast in your hotel and check out. You will be transferred to airport where you will board a flight back to India.
