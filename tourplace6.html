Malaysia & Singapore 6 Days & 5 Nights

Day wise Itinerary

Day 1

AARRIVAL ► PUTRAJAYA TOUR ► KUALA LUMPUR CITY TOUR

You will be greeted by our representative as soon as you arrive at the Kaula Lumpur International Airport. After pick up from airport en route visit Putrajaya, the federal administrative center of Malaysia. Influenced by the Iranian architecture, you could see marvelously sculptured Government center and mosque, along with some rare panoramic sights. Putrajaya built as a model garden city is sophisticated with the information networks based on multimedia technologies and is so called the first Intelligent Garden City of Malaysia. It is considered to be one of the best well planned cities better known for its architectural landscape, capable enough to woo your thoughts. Later get back to the hotel in Kaula Lumpur, check in and relax. An interesting half day city tour which unveils the beauty and charm of the old and new KualaLumpur – Garden City of Lights. See the contrast of the magnificent skyscrapers and the buildings of the colonial days. Highlights of the tour: Photo stop at the King's Palace, the National Monument, the National Mosque, and Independence Square. KL Tower Kuala Lumpur comes alive in a blaze of multi-colored lights when night falls. We will fascinate you with a breathtaking view of Kuala lumpur from tallest telecommunication tower in Southeast Asia ► TICKETS INCLUDED → KL GALLERY & KL TOWER

Day 2

GENTING - BATU CAVES TOUR ► TICKETS INCLUDED → CABLE CAR

After breakfast at the hotel, proceed to Genting Highland. Stop at Batu Caves which host the largest Lord Muruga Statue for a Photo shoot on the way to Genting. Our next stop will be at the strawberry Farm before reaching Ge. ting Highlands Genting Highlands is situated 2000 meters above sea level & is tagged 'City of Entertainment' due to its range of entertainment. Unique Experiences Snow World, Genting Sky Venture, X-pedition Wall, Ripley’s Believe it or Not Museum and Genting International Casinos. All aged below 21 years are restricted from entering the casino. Return back to Kuala Lumpur. ► TICKETS INCLUDED → CABLE CAR

Day 3

KUALA LUPUR ►SINGAPORE VIP COACH TRANSFER

After breakfast at the hotel, check out and proceed to Singapore. En route, pass by oil palm and rubber plantations - enjoy the rural areas of Malaysia. Upon arrival, Check-in to hotel. Evening, start your Singapore adventure with the Night Safari , experience the fiery Thumbuakar Tribal Performance and an exciting Tram Safari Adventure. You would also get to watch the Creatures of the Night show - an amazing show by the nocturnal animals. ► TICKETS INCLUDED → NIGHT SAFARI

Day 4

BIRD PARK ► SENTOSA ISLAND TOUR

After enjoying a scrumptious breakfast at the hotel, you would be picked up from the hotel lobby and taken to Jurong BIRD PARK which is a 20. 2 hectare open-concept park. It is the largest in the Asia Pacific and the best in the world. Its collection of more than 9, 000 birds from 600 species is among the largest in the world. The Park specialises in birds from South Asia. The Park has four aviaries, among which is the world’s largest walk-in aviary with the tallest man-made waterfall for visitors to enjoy a close-up view of free-flying birds from Africa and South American Birds, staged at the Pool Amphitheatre and Fuji Hawk Proceed for the Sentosa Island Tour after the city tour. SENTOSA ISLAND TOUR - Here you can enjoy a cable car ride, S. E. A. Aquarium, Luge a fun outdoor experience for the entire family, Skyride the Skyride chairlift offers panoramic views of Sentosa Island, Singapore’s skyline and South China and Wings of Time a spectacular night show of water, laser and fire effects, set against the backdrop of the open sea. The tour would conclude at around 8. 30 pm, after which you could retire to the hotel. ► TICKETS INCLUDED → BIRD PARK S. E. A. AQUARIUM, WINGS OF TIME, LUGE & SKYRIDE

Day 6

HALF-DAY CITY TOUR OF SINGAPORE - DEPARTURE

After enjoying a scrumptious breakfast at the hotel, you would be picked up from the hotel lobby and taken for an exciting half-day city tour of Singapore on a seat-in-coach basis. The half-day tour of this vibrant city would begin with a visit to the Gardens by the Bay - rated amongst the top 10 indoor gardens in the world; this award winning attraction is renowned all over the world for its beauty, serenity and pure innovation. Then you would be taken to the Merlion Statue – a statue with the head of a lion and body of a fish; it is one of the landmarks of Singapore. You would then stop at China Town – a historic market filled with traditional shops as well as equable stores and modern cafes. Here you can shop for reasonably priced trinkets and souvenirs and feast on the local delicacies. You would also be visiting the Jewellery Factory.
