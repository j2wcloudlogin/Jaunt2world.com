8 Days Sightseeing Tour Of Nepal

Day 1

Arrival in Kathmandu

You will be welcomed in the city of marvellous culture with a welcome drink while you are transferred to a hotel.

Day 2

Sightseeing in Kathmandu

After breakfast, you will be led to a Buddhist Stupa where you can attend their morning prayers, visit the green city of Bhaktapur where you can try your hands at yogurt making and pottery making at Durbar Square

Enjoy delicious dinner and cultural dance performance at Patan before heading back to the hotel

Day 3

Chitwan National Park

Travel to Chitwan on the third day and relax for the rest of the hours.

Day 4

Explore Chitwan National Park

Day four is dedicated to exploring Chitwan National Park, which is Asiaâ€™s finest game parks.

You can spot animals like rhinoceros, sloth bears and striped hyenas among the many others. A jungle ride or elephant safari can be the perfect end to your day at this famous park.

Day 5

Leave for Pokhara

Reach the land that highlights the Annapurna range with five mountains that are at the height of 7200m alongside the Machhapuchare Mountain standing tall at the altitude of 7000m.

Use this day to relax until evening, and walk around its market that is set by the lake. You can also savour some of their local delicacies

Day 6

Boating on Lake Phewa

This day begins with a breath-taking view of the sunrise which you will capture a scenic view of the Annapurna Range from a World Peace Pagoda on a hilltop. Walk around the Raniban Forest along with the guide before you take a boat ride at Lake Phewa.

The day will end with a visit to a Tibetan refugee camp and a local school where you can watch the prayers made by the monks.

Day 7

You will fly back to Kathmandu in the morning after which you will find the renowned Buddhist Swayambhunath stupa. A walk to the Durbar Square follows which houses fifty shrines and temples.

You will also take a rickshaw ride from Hanuman Dhoka to Thamel to get a close look at the life of the people in Nepal. After exploring the Thamel streets on foot, we will drive you back to the hotel for your last night in Nepal.

Day 8

Departure from Nepal

Spend the morning in leisure activities or shopping before we drop you off to the airport to catch an afternoon flight.
